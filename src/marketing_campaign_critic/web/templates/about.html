About Critic.ai

The Challenge

Small businesses often struggle with getting diverse feedback on their marketing campaigns. Traditional methods are expensive and time-consuming. Our tool simplifies this by providing rapid, AI-driven feedback.

Technology Stack

  • CrewAI: Automates AI simulations for diverse demographic feedback in real-time.
  • Cerebras: Uses high-performance compute to handle complex AI models and run simulations faster.
  • Flask: Powers the backend, handling user requests, data processing, and AI models integration.
  • Python: The core language for machine learning models and backend development.

How It Works

Simply input the details of your marketing campaign, define your target audience, and receive personalized feedback based on demographic insights. The tool helps you quickly identify what works and areas that need improvement.
